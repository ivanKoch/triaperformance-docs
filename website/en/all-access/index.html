Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — the membership runs directly through TrainingPeaks Payments, so you can cancel from there whenever you want.

How do I get access after subscribing?

TrainingPeaks automatically connects you to my coach profile, activates your TrainingPeaks Premium, and gives you access to the plans included in the membership. Members Area access arrives separately as soon as you subscribe.

Is this the same as 1:1 coaching?

No — All-Access is self-guided: you choose and run your own plans, with Premium and the guides included. If you'd rather have personalized testing and weekly adjustments from a coach, 1:1 coaching is the other option.
